      The Future of E-Commerce does not look like E-Commerce



      Shopping behaviour is changing fast together with technology. Yet most websites don’t represent this change: they still look like digital catalogs with product descriptions. That’s about to change!


      Recently I shared my research on the future of luxury e-commerce in Milan at Huawei’s Experience Innovation Summit and with the LVMH Experience Design Community in Paris. I wanted to share this more widely in order to share a glimpse of the future of shopping I see emerging.


      As you will see if you dig into my presentation, in the end, the future of luxury e-commerce isn’t about adding more features. It’s about building experiences that empower a quest for meaning, stay rooted in humanity and craftsmanship, and let tech and design quietly disappear into the background.
